TRACES

What reminds you?

Drug addiction affects many in our community. Whether you've lost a family member to a drug overdose, lost touch with a friend who uses, or are fighting your own addiction, daily reminders—objects, places, people—can be anywhere.

Traces is a collection of those reminders and the feelings they evoke. Tell us your story and help us explore the deep and subtle ways that drug addiction in Vermont leaves its mark.
