What Should I Share?

Traces is an attempt to catalog the people, places and things that remind us of those we've lost to drug addiction. What you share might be deeply personal but otherwise ordinary—an empty chair, a log once used for splitting wood, maybe even a person.

The traces of those we've lost are everywhere. We're asking, what are they?
