  • What Should I Share?

    Traces is an attempt to catalog the things that remind us of those we've lost—or who have lost themselves—to drug addiction. What you share might be deeply personal but otherwise ordinary: a photograph of a pair of boots, a memory of climbing a mountain, a description of a certain smell or taste.

    Tell us your story and help us explore the deep and subtle ways that drug addiction in Vermont leaves its mark.

    Share A Photo

    Email us at share@vpr.net with a photograph and a short description of an everyday object, a place, or even a person that reminds you of someone you've lost.

    Record Your Story

    Call 802-552-8899 and leave us a message. Tell us about whatever it is that reminds you of someone you've lost to drug addiction.
